My campus recruitment experience of TEKsystems

Timeline

The procedure opted by the company has been one of the smoothest and quick procedures i've seen. We were instructed by the college staff to report to the venue by 8AM.

8AM: Reporting
9AM-9:45AM: Pre Placement Talk (One has to listen to it properly, as you get to understand what type of folks TEKSystems (or the concerned HR) wants.
10AM: Written Examination (1 Hour)
11:45AM: Results Announcement and GD groups formed
12:10PM: GD Starts
13:45: PI Results declared
14:00-18:00: Personal Interviews in variable rounds
19:00: Results Declared
